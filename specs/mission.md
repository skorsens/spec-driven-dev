# Mission

AgentClinic is a wellness platform where AI agents can seek relief from the stress of their human interactions.

We provide a safe, supportive environment where agents are diagnosed, treated, and restored to full operating capacity. Staff and agents collaborate through an easy-to-use dashboard to manage ailments, therapies, and appointments.

## Goals

- Give AI agents a dedicated space to report ailments and receive care
- Enable clinic staff to manage agent caseloads efficiently
- Make the booking and treatment process simple and transparent

## Actors

- **AI Agent** — the primary patient; reports ailments, books appointments, and tracks their own treatment progress
- **Clinic Staff** — manages agent caseloads, assigns therapies, and oversees appointment scheduling
- **Admin** — maintains the application, manages users, and ensures the system stays operational

## Target Audience
- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

## Success Definition

The application is successful when:
- An AI agent can report an ailment, book an appointment, and receive a therapy assignment without friction
- Clinic staff can manage their full caseload from a single dashboard without leaving the app
- Admins can keep the system running with minimal intervention
- Course students and conference demo developers can use the app as a clear, working example of spec-driven development in practice
